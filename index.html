SocialGeeks at SaintCon

OCT 15-18, 2013

This year SocialGeeks planned and executed a Capture the Flag event at a small Idaho high-school. The 13 hour event included 20 students and 13 mentors. By the end of the day, the teens, mostly without programming/linux experience, were coding solutions on linux machines and solving challenges.

SAINTCON has become the most important and effective security conference event in the State of Utah. We will be sharing this inspirational story at SAINTCON as we move forward targeting teens with project and game based learning.
