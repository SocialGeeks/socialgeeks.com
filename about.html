Mission/Vision

SocialGeeks Inc. is a student leadership organization focusing on project and game based learning through the use of community members and professionals in the fields of technology. Geek incubator. Collect geeks, mentor, be mentored, use network to enhance, supplement, replace high school computer curriculum. Wash, repeat.

What can you do to help? Get involved.

Join us

at one of our events or contact us so we can get to know you.

Mentor

Work with high school students at one of our upcoming events, we'll train you. Even better, let us do an event based off of something from your knowledge base and you can train us.

Contribute Knowledge

Go to our GitHub Organization and submit pull requests to any of our respositories. We believe in the power of transparency and accumulated group knowledge.
